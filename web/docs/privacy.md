# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 18, 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how Wavox ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The Bot collects and stores the following data:

- **Discord User ID** — to associate listening history and preferences with your account.
- **Song playback data** — track names, artists, and timestamps of songs you play or like.
- **Server ID** — to manage per-server queues and settings.

The Bot does **not** collect:

- Message content (other than commands directed at the Bot)
- Direct messages
- Email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information beyond your Discord User ID

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

- Provide music playback and queue management features
- Track listening history and generate user statistics
- Improve the Bot's functionality

We do **not** use your data for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on the Bot's server in a SQLite database. We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

Your data is **not** sold to or shared with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

The Bot interacts with the following third-party services to provide its functionality. Certain data (such as search queries) is necessarily transmitted to these services as part of normal operation:

- **Discord API** — for bot communication ([Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy))
- **YouTube** — for audio search and playback ([YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms), [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Spotify API** — for track and playlist metadata ([Spotify Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/))

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — request a copy of the data the Bot stores about you.
- **Modify** your data — request corrections to any inaccurate data.
- **Delete** your data — request permanent deletion of all data associated with your account.
- **Opt out** of data collection — you may opt out of listening history tracking. Note that opting out may limit certain features such as user statistics and liked songs.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the methods listed in the Contact section below.

## 6. Applicable Privacy Laws

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection regulations, including:

- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) — EU
- **UK GDPR** — United Kingdom
- **LGPD** (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados) — Brazil
- **CCPA/CPRA** (California Consumer Privacy Act / California Privacy Rights Act) — California, USA

If you are a resident of any of these jurisdictions, you may have additional rights under local law, including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

## 7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as the Bot is operational and you continue to use it. If you request deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction), in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated through the Bot's support server or by updating the "Last updated" date above.

## 10. Disclaimer

Wavox is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Discord Inc., YouTube, SoundCloud, Spotify, or any of their subsidiaries or affiliates.

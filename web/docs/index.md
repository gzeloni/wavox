# Wavox

A powerful, high-performance music bot built to elevate your listening experience.
Stream your favorite tracks seamlessly from YouTube, SoundCloud, and Spotify with fast response times and crystal-clear audio playback.

**Clean. Fast. Reliable.**
*Your soundtrack, amplified.*

---

## Features

- **Multi-platform** — play from YouTube, SoundCloud, and Spotify
- **Crystal-clear audio** — high-quality playback with low latency
- **Queue management** — add, skip, shuffle, loop, and reorder tracks
- **Spotify integration** — supports tracks, albums, and playlists
- **Now playing** — rich embeds with track info and playback controls
- **Reactions** — like songs with a heart reaction on the now playing card
- **User stats** — track your listening history and favorite songs

---

## Quick Start

1. [Invite the bot](https://wavox.gzeloni.dev/invite/) to your server
2. Join a voice channel
3. Type `!!play <song name or URL>`

---

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `!!play <query>` | Play a song or add it to the queue |
| `!!queue` | Show the current queue |
| `!!skip` | Skip the current song |
| `!!pause` | Pause playback |
| `!!resume` | Resume playback |
| `!!loop` | Toggle loop mode |
| `!!shuffle` | Shuffle the queue |
| `!!stats` | View your listening stats |
